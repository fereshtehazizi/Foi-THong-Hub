The Bangkok City

Bangkok is a dynamic city where ancient traditions blend effortlessly with modern life. From sacred temples and floating markets to neon-lit streets and luxury malls, the city offers a rich mix of culture, energy, and endless discoveries waiting around every corner.

Dining in Bangkok

Dining in Bangkok is more than just eating it's an adventure. The city is packed with everything from street vendors serving flavorful noodles to elegant rooftop restaurants with panoramic views. Every meal is a chance to experience bold tastes and local creativity.

Bangkok on a Plate

To truly understand Bangkok, you have to taste it. Each dish reflects the city’s history, diversity, and passion for food from spicy curries and fresh herbs to sweet treats and grilled delicacies. It's a journey of flavor in every bite.

Our Team

Garima Arora

Indian | Chef Co-chef of Foi Thong

Chef Garima Arora made history as the first Indian woman to receive a Michelin star. With a background in journalism and training under renowned chefs like René Redzepi, she brings bold creativity to her cuisine. At Foi Thong, she fuses Indian roots with Thai influences in truly unique dishes.

Thomas & Mathias Sühring

German | Both chefs Executive Chefs & Co-Chefs of Foi Thong

Twin brothers Thomas and Mathias Sühring bring a modern interpretation of classic German dishes to Bangkok. With training in some of Europe’s finest kitchens, they combine tradition and innovation in every dish. At Foi Thong, their shared vision turns nostalgia into fine dining artistry.

Thitid Tassanakajohn

Thai | Chef MasterChef & Co-Owner

Chef Ton is a leading figure in modern Thai cuisine, known for blending local ingredients with contemporary techniques. He graduated from the Culinary Institute of America and worked in Michelin-starred restaurants in New York. His mission is to showcase the richness of Thai flavors on a world-class stage.

Pim Techamuanvivit

Thai | Chef MasterChef of Foi Thong

Chef Pim is a Thai-born chef who returned from the U.S. to lead Foi Thong, one of Bangkok’s most celebrated restaurants. With a background in food writing and fine dining, she’s passionate about preserving traditional Thai flavors. Her cooking honors heritage while delivering exceptional modern quality.
